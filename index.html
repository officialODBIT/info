About ODBIT

ODBIT is a Roblox-based service dedicated to combating sex crimes, pedophilia, rape, and online dating. Roblox's Terms of Service explicitly forbid online dating, and ODBIT works to enforce these rules to maintain a safe and appropriate environment for all users.

Our team consists of experts who monitor, report, and take action against violations that threaten the safety of players. We utilize advanced detection methods, community reports, and collaborate with moderation teams to ensure offenders face consequences. ODBIT strives to protect young users from exploitation and prevent harmful behaviors from spreading within the Roblox community.

Our services include monitoring suspicious activities, investigating reports of inappropriate behavior, and educating players about online safety. ODBIT believes in a proactive approach—by identifying and reporting dangerous users before they can cause harm, we help Roblox remain a safe space for everyone.

Through our efforts, we have successfully identified and reported numerous accounts engaged in illegal or unethical behavior. We work closely with authorities when necessary and continually improve our strategies to keep up with emerging threats. We stand firm in our commitment to protecting the Roblox community from predators and ensuring a safe and fun gaming experience for all.
